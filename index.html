About

The SVPMS Innovation Cell at College of Engineering, Malegaon BK is a student-led organization that focuses on fostering innovation and finding innovative solutions to social problems.
The cell provides a platform for students to channel their creativity and entrepreneurial spirit towards addressing various societal issues.
The primary objective of the SVPMS Innovation Cell is to encourage students to think beyond the traditional academic curriculum and apply their knowledge and skills to real-world challenges. The cell organizes a wide range of activities and initiatives to promote innovation and problem-solving among students. Some of the activities carried out by the SVPM'S Innovation Cell include:
1. Innovation Challenges
2. Workshops and Seminars
3. Incubation Support
4. Community Engagement
5. Collaboration and Networking
Overall, the SVPMS Innovation Cell at College of Engineering, Malegaon BK plays a crucial role in nurturing an innovative mindset among students and empowering them to make a positive impact on society. It provides a platform for students to combine their technical skills, creativity, and social consciousness to develop innovative solutions for social problems.
